About Arena LoL Win Tracker

Arena LoL Win Tracker – Complete the Arena God Challenge Efficiently

Arena LoL Win Tracker is a free, lightweight tool designed to help League of Legends players track their victories in Arena mode and efficiently complete the Arena God challenge. Mark the champions you’ve won with, monitor your remaining picks, and organize your progress in seconds.

Why Use This Tracker?

Completing the Arena God challenge requires winning with 160+ champions. Without tracking, it’s easy to forget who you’ve already used. This tool helps you:

  • Identify champions you still need to win with
  • Avoid repeating already completed champions
  • Plan your next Arena sessions strategically
  • Track progress clearly and efficiently

Core Features

  • ✔ Mark victories for each champion
  • ✔ Filter by won or not-won champions
  • ✔ Instant champion search
  • ✔ Sort alphabetically or by win status
  • ✔ Export and import progress (JSON format)
  • ✔ Multi-language support (English & Spanish)
  • ✔ Fully responsive for desktop and mobile

Privacy & Data

Your progress is stored locally in your browser. No accounts, no logins, and no personal data collection. You can export your progress at any time to back it up or move it to another device.

Designed for Arena Mode Players

Whether you're a casual player aiming to complete the challenge gradually or a competitive player optimizing every session, this Arena LoL Tracker helps you stay organized and focused. Save time, track smarter, and reach the Arena God title faster.
